# Northstar Studio Market Analysis (2026-02-15)

## Objective
Pick digital products with strong buyer intent for a template-based storefront and avoid low-intent novelty products.

## Signals Used
1. Etsy marketplace scale and active buyer base indicate strong demand for template-driven, practical digital goods.
2. Etsy marketplace insights tool highlights active search behavior and category-level demand patterns.
3. Etsy trend report themes (self-improvement, practical life optimization, events) map well to planners, productivity, and event templates.
4. Notion and Canva platform ecosystems confirm large, active template consumption behavior.

## Product Direction Chosen
- Creator Growth: caption systems, hook libraries, UGC assets
- Creator/Small-Biz Ops: SOP manuals, launch planners, client packs
- Career and Income Tools: resume/interview kits
- Ecommerce and Marketplace Optimization: Etsy SEO toolkit
- Home and Lifestyle Utility: meal prep and family routine planners
- Event/Hospitality Templates: wedding suite, Airbnb welcome guide

## Why This Mix
- These categories solve repeat, high-friction problems where templates reduce time and uncertainty.
- The products are easy to preview before purchase, improving conversion confidence.
- The catalog spans multiple buyer segments to reduce single-niche risk while keeping fulfillment digital.

## Notes
- Product selection is an inference from listed sources plus storefront strategy.
- For deeper validation, next iteration should include conversion analytics by category and A/B pricing tests.
